Sales Performance Dashboards

Overview

Power BI dashboards built for leadership and regional sales teams to monitor sales performance, dealer contribution, and stock movement.

Business Problem

Sales reports were manually prepared by different teams, leading to inconsistent metrics and delayed insights.

What Was Built

• Executive summary dashboards
• Dealer and region-wise analysis
• Month-on-month growth metrics

Impact

Enabled faster decision-making during monthly sales reviews and significantly reduced manual reporting effort.
