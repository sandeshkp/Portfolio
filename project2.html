Sales Performance Dashboard & Analytics Platform

A centralized analytics system built to replace fragmented, email-driven reporting with real-time, hierarchy-based business insights across India.

1. Project Context

The organization operates with a complex, nationwide sales hierarchy consisting of Dealers reporting to Area Sales Managers (ASMs), ASMs reporting to Regional Sales Managers (RSMs), and regional leadership overseeing performance across multiple states. The product portfolio spans a wide range of categories and subcategories, making data consolidation and reporting inherently complex.

2. Challenges Identified

• Sales, stock, and production data existed in multiple disconnected formats

• Depot-wise stock, cut cases, and launch-pending reports were emailed individually

• Large image-based reports caused mail server load and storage issues

• Month-end PPTs were manually prepared by sales teams, impacting daily operations

3. Solution Approach

A lightweight Flask-based analytical application was developed, supported by a heavy-duty backend ETL pipeline. The frontend focuses purely on data consumption and visualization, while all computationally intensive processing is handled upstream.

Automation-first design: zero manual intervention after daily data arrival.

4. Data Pipeline & Architecture

Every morning, previous-day sales, stock, supply chain, and production data is received via FTP from SAP HANA systems. This data undergoes validation, transformation, aggregation, and enrichment as part of an automated ETL workflow.

• SAP HANA → FTP ingestion

• Automated transformation & validation

• Generation of 100+ analytical reports (including image mapping)

• Storage in MySQL and shared servers

5. Platform Capabilities

Sales Analytics Stock Visibility Supply Chain Tracking Production Insights FY-wise History

Users access the finalized analytical outputs through the Flask application, eliminating dependency on emails and static presentations. Over time, selective operational windows such as stock updates and allotment views were introduced to enhance usability without compromising analytical performance.

6. Business Impact

The platform significantly reduced reporting time, infrastructure overhead, and manual effort. Stakeholders now access near real-time insights instead of waiting for month-end summaries, enabling proactive decision-making at all levels of the sales hierarchy.

Result: Faster decisions · Lower operational load · Consistent metrics · Scalable analytics

7. UI / UX Representation

Only representative, non-sensitive mock screens are used to demonstrate navigation, filters, and report structure. No proprietary data, metrics, or visuals are exposed.
